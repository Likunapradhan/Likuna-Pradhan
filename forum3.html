How to Improve Urban Green Spaces in Your City?

Urban green spaces are essential for enhancing the quality of life in cities. Here are some strategies to improve them:

  • Plant more trees and shrubs in public areas.
  • Create community gardens and green roofs.
  • Implement park clean-up drives and maintenance.
