Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about EcoTrack and our services.

What is EcoTrack?

EcoTrack is a web-based platform designed to help individuals monitor, understand, and reduce their carbon footprint through daily activity tracking, personalized tips, and data visualization.

How does EcoTrack help with sustainability?

EcoTrack provides users with personalized recommendations based on their daily activities, visualizes their carbon footprint over time, and encourages sustainable living by tracking progress and providing actionable tips.

How can I get started with EcoTrack?

You can get started by signing up on our website, setting up your profile, and beginning to track your daily activities. Our platform will guide you through the process and provide personalized tips based on your data.

Is there a cost associated with using EcoTrack?

EcoTrack offers both free and premium plans. The free plan provides basic features, while the premium plan includes advanced features and additional tools for a more comprehensive sustainability experience.

Can I access EcoTrack on mobile devices?

Yes, EcoTrack is designed to be fully responsive and can be accessed on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops. You can track your activities and access your data from any device with an internet connection.

How can I contact support if I have more questions?

If you have additional questions or need assistance, you can contact our support team through the contact form on our website or email us at support@ecotrack.com.
